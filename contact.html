Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn shoes in original packaging. Free return shipping is provided for all orders over $100. Please visit our returns page for detailed instructions.

How do I find the right size?

Use our comprehensive size guide which includes measurements in both centimeters and inches. We also offer virtual fitting technology and free size exchanges if needed.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide to over 150 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Free international shipping is available for orders over $200.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order status by logging into your account on our website.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for qualified retailers. Please contact our sales team at sales@stepforward.com with your business information for more details.
