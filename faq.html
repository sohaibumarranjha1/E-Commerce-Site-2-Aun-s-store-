Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within Pakistan. Express shipping (1-2 days) and international shipping (7-14 days) are also available. All orders placed before 2 PM ship the same day.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over PKR 5,000. For orders under this amount, standard shipping is PKR 250.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 150 countries worldwide. International shipping costs vary by destination and package weight. Free international shipping is available for orders over $200.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn shoes in original condition. Returns are free for orders over PKR 5,000, and we provide prepaid return labels.

How do I start a return?

Log into your account, go to order history, and click "Return Items" next to your order. We'll email you a prepaid return label and instructions.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes! Size exchanges are free and processed quickly. We'll send out your new size as soon as we receive your return.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days once we receive your return. You'll get an email confirmation when your refund is processed.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my shoe size?

Use our comprehensive size guide to measure your feet properly. We recommend measuring in the evening when your feet are slightly swollen for the most accurate fit.

Do your shoes run true to size?

Most of our shoes run true to size, but some styles may vary. Check the product page for specific sizing notes and customer reviews for fit feedback.

What if I'm between sizes?

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for athletic shoes and sizing down for dress shoes. Contact our customer service for personalized advice.

Payment & Orders

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders.

Is it safe to order online?

Yes, our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never store your credit card details on our servers.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, contact our customer service team and we'll do our best to help.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get 15% off with valid student ID verification. Military personnel and healthcare workers also receive special discounts.

Product Care

How should I clean my shoes?

Cleaning depends on the material. Leather shoes should be wiped with a damp cloth and conditioned regularly. Athletic shoes can be machine washed on gentle cycle. Check the care label for specific instructions.

How can I make my shoes last longer?

Rotate between multiple pairs, use shoe trees to maintain shape, keep them clean and dry, and store them properly. Avoid wearing the same pair daily.

Do you offer shoe repair services?

We don't offer direct repair services, but we can recommend authorized repair shops in your area. Some repairs may be covered under our warranty.

Account & Profile

Do I need an account to place an order?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save favorites, and enjoy faster checkout for future purchases.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address in your account settings. You'll need to verify the new email address for security purposes.
