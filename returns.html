Return Conditions

Please ensure your items meet these requirements

Items We Accept

  • Shoes in original, unworn condition
  • Items with original packaging and tags
  • Returns within 30 days of delivery
  • Items purchased at full price or on sale
  • Gift purchases with gift receipt
  • Defective or damaged items

Items We Cannot Accept

  • Worn shoes with signs of use
  • Items without original packaging
  • Returns after 30-day period
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items damaged by customer misuse
  • Final sale or clearance items

Returns FAQ

Common questions about returns and exchanges

Can I return shoes I've worn once?

We only accept returns for shoes that are in new, unworn condition. However, if you're unsatisfied with the quality or comfort, please contact our customer service team to discuss your options.

What if I lost the original box?

While we prefer returns in original packaging, we'll still accept your return if you no longer have the box. Just pack the shoes securely in any appropriate container.

Can I exchange for a different style?

Style exchanges are treated as returns and new purchases. You'll receive a full refund for your return and can place a new order for the style you prefer.

Do you offer international returns?

Yes, we accept international returns. However, customers are responsible for return shipping costs from international locations. Please contact us for specific instructions.
